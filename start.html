Workout Class

Programs all in to one of three categories. Strength workouts will push your muscles to their limits and aim to increase your lift capacity for the next workout. The Agility program is focused on pumping your ability to move. Fast-twitch training, vertical travel, conditioning are all advantages in sport and they are also the nucleus of this type of training. The Other class is reserved for special workouts that focus specific goals.
