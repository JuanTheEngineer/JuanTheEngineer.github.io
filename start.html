Workout Class

Programs all in to one of three categories. The first one is Strength. Strength workouts will push your muscles to their limits and aim to increase your lift capacity for the next workout. Then there is Agility. With Agility program is focused on pumping your ability to move. Fast-twitch training, vertical travel, conditioning are all advantages in sport and they are also the nucleus of this type of training. The last class, Other, is reserved for special workouts tht focus specific goals.
